About me

I am starting this simple blog to describe some of my cheap’n’dirty hacks and diy projects that I find useful enough and worth sharing. When describing projects as “cheap”, I consider that you have fun building it and your time commitment is out of the equation :)

You might find the affiliate links to the products that I have used personally and/or recommend to be used for one or another DIY project. All components used for the projects were purchased by me at full retail price.
